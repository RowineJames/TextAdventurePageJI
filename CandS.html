Story Hooks

Who is the player?

A student and collector of the weird and bizarre.

What is the setting?

Victorian London, shrouded in superstition, between the extinction of old-world magic and new-world industry, in the protagonist's SHOPPE OF CURIOSITIES.

What is the threat?

A curse, and sickness, affecting the most impoverished of all London rookeries, St. Giles' Rats' Nest. At least according to the shop's latest visitor.

What is the goal?

To assess the latest visitor and decide if the case is worth taking up.
